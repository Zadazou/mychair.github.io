Tonico Sofa is designed by Sergio Rodrigues in 1963. The large roll supported by leather strapes on the top is a comfortable solution to rest the head.

Sergio Rodrigues(1927-2014) was a Brazilian architecture and designer. Rodrigues was one of the the pioneers to transform the Brazilian design in industrial design and make it known worldwide.

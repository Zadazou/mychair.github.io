MT3 Armchair by Ron Arad

Designed by Ron Arad in 2004.The initials of the pieces, "MT", are pronounced as "empty" meaning "empty" and the gap is really the key element if the project.The volume, made through a rotational molding, is in fact carved and shows provocatively the inside, finished in different colors.

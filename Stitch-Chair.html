Stitching Concrete by Florian Schmid

Home 01 02 03 04 05 06 07 08 09

Industrial design graduate Florian Schmid made these stools by folding fabric that's impregnated with cement then drenching it in water. The stools are called Stiching Concrete. They are made of a material called concrete canvas. It consists of cement layered between fabric and a PVC backing. Once soaked it can be manipulated for a few hours before hardening.
